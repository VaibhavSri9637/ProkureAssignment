Product





Prokure is an online wholesale marketplace where retailers can discover and purchase goods directly from the manufacturers or large suppliers.

India retail market is one of the top 10 in the world by economics value. Nearly 92% of the retail in India happens in unorganized sector and this sector is highly fragmented and non-transparent. The supply chain in unorganized retail is broken. This leads to High cost of goods sold, High cost of supply chain, Limited product discovery, Limited product availability etc. together which makes the retail in India one of the most inefficient forms of retail worldwide.

Prokure is a technology solution to fix the broken supply chain. Using Prokure app retailers in every category can discover and order all the products in that category. By connecting the retailers directly to the manufacturers and wholesalers, we are eliminating a lot of middle layers in the supply chain leading to significant price reduction. Deliveries are fulfilled by third party.
